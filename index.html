The Leela Mahal, Jaipur

Relish the sumptuous offerings that have led to this property’s ranking as Asia’s Best City Hotel. From its design and decor, from gastronomy to hospitality, this magnificent, modern palace is a true reflection of the city’s multifaceted heritage. Its location mere minutes from the airport and Lutyens’ Delhi as well as its undertaking of the Suraksha initiative towards health and hygiene makes this 5-star hotel the ultimate choice for city explorers, history buffs and frequent fliers alike.
